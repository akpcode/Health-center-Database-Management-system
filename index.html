ADMIN

They are resposible for organizing and overseeing the health services and daily activities of the clinic facilities.

NURSING DEPARTMENT

Our nurses works throughout the intake of a patient carrying out all the questionings, symptoms, medical histories which provides the doctor with a complete understanding of patient's current medical needs and bacground

In most cases they provide direct care to patients which includes:

Administering treatments
Treat and change the dressing on a wound
Apply topical treatments via intravenous methods or direct injection.

DOCTOR

Our Doctor are professionals which are always provide the best services for our patients some of which includes:

  • Undertaking patients consultations and physical examinations.
  • Providing general pre-and post operative care.
  • Assessing and planning treatment requirements.
  • Providing treatment and drugs for the sickness diagonised.
